My Projects

IrriMagic

IrriMagic is an IoT-based project with an Android interface designed to revolutionize the way we manage irrigation systems. This project integrates various sensors and actuators to monitor soil moisture levels, weather conditions, and other environmental factors. The Android app provides a user-friendly interface to control and automate the irrigation process, ensuring optimal water usage and improving crop yield.

SavourCraft

SavourCraft is a restaurant-based webpage designed to enhance the dining experience for customers. The website features an intuitive layout with detailed menus, reservation options, and customer reviews. It also includes a backend system for restaurant management, allowing staff to update menus, manage reservations, and track customer feedback efficiently.

Sahayak

Are you a senior citizen looking for comprehensive healthcare solutions? Introducing Sahayak, the web application designed to be your one-stop-shop for all your needs. Sahayak is more than just another healthcare platform - it's a community-driven solution that caters to the unique requirements of the elderly. With Sahayak, you can easily access a wide range of features that will simplify your life. Book appointments with healthcare professionals, receive personalized nutrition tips, and discover a vibrant community of likeminded individuals. But that's not all - Sahayak also provides a platform for volunteers to offer their services, empowering them to make a meaningful difference in the lives of senior citizens. Don't let the challenges of aging weigh you down. Sahayak is here to be your trusted companion, ensuring that you can maintain your independence and live a fulfilling life.
